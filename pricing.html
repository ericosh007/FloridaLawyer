Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between Free and Pro?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. No long-term contracts. Your existing work is always saved.

What happens if I hit my Free tier limits?

You'll see a notification to upgrade to Pro. Your existing work remains accessible, but you won't be able to analyze new documents or generate new filings until you upgrade or wait for the monthly reset.

Do limits reset monthly?

Yes. Document analysis and generation limits reset on your billing date each month.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Attorney-client privilege is maintained. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your data with third parties.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards via Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover).

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. No cancellation fees. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer discounts for multiple users?

Contact us at mark@osherowpllc.com for firm pricing and volume discounts.
