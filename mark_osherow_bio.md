## Full Biography

### Overview

Mark R. Osherow is a Florida Bar Board Certified Specialist in Business Litigation, a distinction held by fewer than 7% of eligible Florida attorneys. Since 2006, he has maintained this certification through rigorous recertification every five years—a testament to his continued excellence in complex commercial litigation.

With over 35 years of experience practicing in both state and federal courts throughout Florida, Mark has served as first-chair in countless trials, arbitrations, and mediations. He has taken innumerable depositions and serves as a trusted legal advisor to clients navigating high-stakes business disputes across a wide range of industries.

Mark's practice encompasses a comprehensive range of business and commercial litigation matters:

- **Trade Secret & Non-Compete Litigation** — Protecting proprietary information and enforcing restrictive covenants

- **Shareholder & Partnership Disputes** — Corporate dissolution, derivative actions, and ownership conflicts
- **Real Estate & Construction Litigation** — Title disputes, construction defects, and commercial lease disputes
- **Securities Litigation** — FINRA arbitration, broker-dealer disputes, and investment fraud
- **Labor & Employment Law** — Title VII, FLSA, ADA, FMLA, and trade secret claims

- **Professional Liability** — Legal and accounting malpractice defense
- **Data Security & Cyber Litigation** — Data breach, cyber defamation, and electronic evidence disputes
