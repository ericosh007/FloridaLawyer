Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Florida Lawyer AI, pricing, security, and features.

Can I switch between Free and Pro?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. No long-term contracts. Your existing work is always saved.

What happens if I hit my Free tier limits?

You'll see a notification to upgrade to Pro. Your existing work remains accessible, but you won't be able to analyze new documents or generate new filings until you upgrade or wait for the monthly reset.

Do limits reset monthly?

Yes. Document analysis and generation limits reset on your billing date each month.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Attorney-client privilege is maintained. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your data with third parties.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards via Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover).

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. No cancellation fees. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

How does Florida Lawyer AI differ from CoCounsel or Harvey AI?

Florida Lawyer AI is built specifically for Florida law with 70KB+ of Florida-specific legal knowledge, automatic element detection, and strategic weakness analysis. We're also more affordable ($99/month vs $225-400 for CoCounsel or $500+ for Harvey AI) and offer a free tier for solo practitioners.

What types of documents can I analyze?

You can analyze complaints, answers, motions, discovery requests, and any other legal filings. We support PDF and Word documents.

What types of documents can I generate?

You can generate 14+ document types including: Motion to Dismiss, Answer, Affirmative Defenses, Counterclaim, Interrogatories, Requests for Production, Requests for Admission, Discovery Objections, Motion to Compel, Motion for Summary Judgment, Motion for Sanctions, Demand Letter, Spoliation Notice, and Engagement Letter.

Can I edit generated documents?

Yes, all generated documents are fully editable. You can download them as Word documents and customize as needed before filing.

How accurate are the Florida citations?

Every citation is drawn from our 70KB+ Florida legal knowledge base, which includes Florida case law, statutes, and jury instructions. We use Claude Sonnet 4.5 with specialized prompts to minimize hallucinated citations.
